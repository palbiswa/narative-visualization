USA State Wise Covid-19 impact and it's recovery through vaccination.

This narative visualization will show COVID-19 impact across US states from January 2020 to July 2021 and how it is recovering from the pandemic.

It will show state wise Covid-19 Cases, state wide Covid Deaths and state wide Vaccination progress in three separate Scenes towords it recovery over time.

The first Scene will show the average covid cases reported from January 2020 to July 2021

The second Scene will show the average covid deaths impacted each state from January 2020 to July 2021

The third Scene will show covid vaccination progress as on July 2021 and how each states is moving towords recovery through vaccinations.Here you may explore vaccination progress in different catagories/paths to get state-wise progress.

These catagories/paths are:

  • How many Doses Administered
  • How many People Vaccinated
  • How many Completed Vaccination
  • How many Completed One Dose Vaccination
  • How many CDC Doses Distributed
